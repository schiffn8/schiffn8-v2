## Context

The Museum of Russian Art (MORA) is an independent institution in Minneapolis dedicated to Russian art, culture, and history — spanning Muscovite Russia, the Russian Empire, the Soviet Union, and its successor states.

The rebranding challenge was fundamentally one of perception: MORA's existing identity undersold the institution. The museum holds serious work and serves a genuine curatorial mission, but its visual presence communicated neither.

The brief was to create an identity that could hold its own alongside peer institutions — minimal, typographically rigorous, and confident enough to let the art lead.
