## Context

Vasaloppet USA is a Nordic ski park in Mora, Minnesota, hosting year-round events — including a signature winter triathlon combining running, biking, and skiing.

[TODO: What's the origin of this project? Was Vasaloppet USA the actual client, or was this a personal rebranding exercise? The design challenge: create an identity that captures the character of cold-weather Nordic sport without defaulting to the generic alpine aesthetic. The inspiration was the Swedish Dalahäst — the iconic carved horse — and the horizontal sweep of a winter landscape at dawn.

### Identity

The wordmark draws from the Dalahäst silhouette — not literally, but in the weight and stance of the letterforms. The color palette is cold: deep navy, ice white, with a single warm accent that references the painted horse's traditional red.
