## Context

Richfield, Minnesota — "The Urban Hometown" — is the state's oldest suburb, directly south of Minneapolis. It has a real identity tension: dense enough to feel urban, residential enough to feel suburban, and perpetually overshadowed by the cities it borders.

The brief was to design a civic identity that acknowledged that tension honestly — not by pretending Richfield is something it isn't, but by reframing what it actually is: a historic, walkable, genuinely diverse community that's been underrepresented by its own visual identity.
