## Context

Tincsure is a medicinal mushroom tincture brand. The brief was to create a packaging system that communicated the brand's wellness positioning without falling into the overcrowded "earthy neutrals + serif" aesthetic that dominates the supplement space.

The brand's visual foundation is Pointillist — individual dots accumulating into a whole, directly mirroring how the product is meant to work: small daily steps composing into sustained well-being.

### Identity and Illustration

The Pointillist artwork is the signature move — each SKU has distinct dot-pattern artwork representing the species: Cordyceps, Turkey Tail, Lion's Mane. The illustrations are dense enough to read as texture from a distance, legible as art up close.

<Image src={illustration} alt="Tincsure — Cordyceps illustration" />

### Packaging System

Three SKUs, unified system. Each box holds a tincture bottle, dosing card, and product insert. The color differentiation between SKUs is subtle — the dot density and hue shift, not a color swap — which keeps the line reading as a family rather than three unrelated products.
