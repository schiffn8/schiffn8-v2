## Context

"High Enough?" is a data visualization editorial piece examining the scale of America's drug epidemic — specifically fentanyl contamination and overdose statistics.

The challenge with data about a crisis is that statistics alone don't move people. A number like "80,000 overdose deaths in a year" is too large to feel real. The editorial goal was to find the visual language that makes the scale land — naturalistic rather than clinical, visceral rather than bureaucratic.
