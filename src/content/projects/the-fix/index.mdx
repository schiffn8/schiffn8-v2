## Approach

### Identity

The campaign identity anchors on yellow — high visibility, universally readable as a signal color, distinct from the clinical blue-white of pharmaceutical packaging. The wordmark is set tight, no decoration. The voice is direct: "Be Prepared, Save Lives."

### The Kit

The physical intervention kit pairs the awareness campaign with action — naloxone, fentanyl test strips, and instructions in one package. The design problem was making it feel approachable rather than clinical, so people would keep it rather than throw it away.
